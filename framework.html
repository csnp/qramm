Why All Four Dimensions Are Essential

Organizations often focus on just one or two areas, leaving critical gaps that undermine their entire quantum readiness effort. True resilience requires excellence across all four dimensions.

Without CVI: Flying Blind

Unknown vulnerabilities, failed migrations, wasted resources, and emergency responses when quantum threats materialize.

Without SGRM: Initiative Failure

Lack of resources, fragmented efforts, regulatory exposure, and competitive disadvantage.

Without DPE: Data Breaches

Future decryption of sensitive data, intellectual property theft, regulatory penalties, and loss of trust.

Without ITR: Failed Deployments

System instabilities, performance problems, extended vulnerabilities, and cost overruns.
