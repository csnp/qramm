The QRAMM Framework

Four integrated dimensions provide comprehensive coverage of quantum readiness, from cryptographic discovery to enterprise-wide implementation.

🔍 Dimension 1: CVI

Cryptographic Visibility & Inventory

Transform invisible crypto dependencies into manageable assets. CVI discovers every algorithm, key, and protocol across your entire infrastructure.

  • Uncover hidden quantum vulnerabilities
  • Map critical dependencies
  • Enable risk-based prioritization

⚖️ Dimension 2: SGRM

Strategic Governance & Risk Management

Elevate quantum security from IT project to business imperative. SGRM ensures executive commitment and sustained resources for multi-year transformation.

  • Secure board-level accountability
  • Integrate with enterprise risk
  • Drive coordinated response

🛡️ Dimension 3: DPE

Data Protection Engineering

Protect against "harvest now, decrypt later" threats. DPE implements quantum-resistant encryption for data that must remain secure for decades.

  • Safeguard long-term sensitive data
  • Deploy quantum-safe encryption
  • Maintain cryptographic agility

⚙️ Dimension 4: ITR

Implementation & Technical Readiness

Bridge the gap between strategy and operational reality. ITR ensures successful deployment without business disruption or performance degradation.

  • Prevent implementation failures
  • Manage complex integrations
  • Optimize operational efficiency
